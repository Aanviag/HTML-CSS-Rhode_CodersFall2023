How to make a sand castle


These are instructions of how to make a sand castle at beaches step by step:

  1. Get a bucket or any cointainer to put the sand
  2. Make sure the sand is a little wet
  3. Then pour sand into the bucket and fill it to the top
  4. Turn the bucket over and place it on the ground
  5. Tap the bucket with a shovel and wait a few seconds
  6. Lastly you can take out the bucket

You can decorate your sand castle to make it look even prettier.You can decorate it with sea shell or anything you find at the beach.
